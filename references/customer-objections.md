# Customer Objections

Use this reference for real customer sales-engineering objections: price, trial, refund, retrofit, old gun compatibility, batch retrofit, and proof.

## "Why Is It More Expensive Than A Normal Gun Or Nozzle?"

Answer logic:

- Acknowledge the concern.
- Explain that the rotary cup head is not a normal nozzle; it targets difficult areas that conventional guns struggle with.
- Reframe the value around reduced rework, better groove coverage, less operator pressure, and better film consistency.

Chinese response:

"你担心价格很正常。这个不是普通喷嘴，它主要解决普通枪在死角、凹槽、深槽、夹角里总差一口气的问题。如果只是喷普通大面，普通枪就够；如果现在的问题是补喷、漏底、膜厚不均、员工反复找角度，那就要按解决问题的价值来判断。"

## "Can I Try It First?"

Answer logic:

- Many customers want to reduce trial risk.
- If direct trial is not offered, recommend sample testing by sending the workpiece.
- Offer video proof during test spraying when possible.

Chinese response:

"如果你担心买回去不适合，可以先把代表性工件寄过来试喷，喷的时候可以拍视频给你看。这样比空口判断更准，也能看清楚凹槽、死角、膜厚和上粉速度。"

## "Can It Fit My Existing Gun?"

Answer logic:

- Most common powder coating gun brands can be adapted.
- Use practical confidence, but still ask for photos if the exact installation matters.
- Mention air supply and simple connection.

Approved wording:

"大多数常见品牌喷枪都可以适配，约 90% 的品牌喷枪可以安装。通常需要给旋转杯头接一路空气，实际安装时按你的枪型和气路配置转接件。"

Ask when needed:

- Gun brand/model.
- Gun head photo.
- Manual gun, automatic gun, or robot/reciprocator.
- Number of units if batch retrofit.

## "Can It Be Used On Robot Or Automatic Stations?"

Answer logic:

- It can be evaluated for robots, reciprocators, and automatic stations.
- Do not ignore mounting, cable, air tube, clearance, weight, and path issues.

Chinese response:

"机器人、自动台、升降机上可以评估使用。关键是看安装空间、气管走线、喷头重量、工件距离和程序路径。如果是多把枪批量改造，建议先装一套验证，再决定整线替换。"

## "Will It Really Save Powder?"

Answer logic:

- The verified field claim may use 8%-12% as an observed saving range.
- Keep it conditional: suitable workpieces and correct process setup.
- Explain why: less powder blown away, less rework, better deposition in corners and grooves.

Chinese response:

"在合适工件和工艺条件下，旋转杯有机会省粉，实际验证中有 8%-12% 左右的省粉空间。但这个不是单靠一个喷头固定保证，还是要看接地、粉房风量、回收系统、粉末、线速和工件形状。更稳妥的判断方式是拿你的工件试喷。"

## "Will It Solve Deep Grooves?"

Answer logic:

- Separate shallow/open grooves from deep/narrow cavities.
- Use a conservative dimension reference.
- Recommend photos and test spraying.

Chinese response:

"浅凹槽、开口槽、箱体夹角这类位置，旋转杯通常更有帮助。深窄槽要看尺寸，约 10mm 宽、80mm 深以内可以作为一个保守参考，但仍要看开口方向和喷头能不能对准。像 10cm 深、1cm 宽的散热片已经属于难喷工件，可以改善，但要保守评估速度和一次成膜效果。"

## "Can It Replace Process Adjustment?"

Answer:

No. It can improve difficult-area coverage and powder cloud behavior, but it cannot replace:

- pretreatment,
- grounding,
- powder quality,
- curing,
- correct electrostatic settings,
- correct line speed,
- correct gun path.

Use this especially when the user's issue is orange peel, pinholes, rework powder not charging, or reclaimed powder problems.

## Batch Retrofit Path

For customers with many guns:

1. Start with one test unit.
2. Verify the hardest workpiece first.
3. Compare coverage, speed, rework, film thickness, and powder use.
4. If the result is stable, expand to more guns or a full automatic/robot station retrofit.

This pattern fits customers who say they have many guns, automatic stations, robot stations, or want to replace several heads at once.

# Nozzle Selection

Use this reference when users ask which rotary cup nozzle to choose. Use English nozzle names, with Chinese explanation when answering Chinese users.

## Flat Nozzle

- Diameter: 50 mm
- Best for: flat panels and large-area coating.
- Typical parts: panels, doors, broad surfaces, sheet-metal faces.
- Use when: the user needs wide surface coverage and film uniformity on open surfaces.
- Caution: not the first choice for narrow grooves or deep recesses.

Chinese wording:

"Flat Nozzle 更适合平板、大面、门板这类开阔表面，主要看膜厚均匀和铺粉效率。"

## Long Rod Nozzle

- Diameter: 15 mm
- Best for: general-purpose coating and deeper access.
- Typical parts: cabinets, deeper grooves, sheet metal, structural parts, welded parts, and many common workpieces.
- Use when: the user is unsure which nozzle to start with, or the part needs reach into a groove or cabinet structure.
- Caution: still confirm groove width, groove depth, and clearance.

Chinese wording:

"Long Rod Nozzle 是比较通用的选择，适合柜体、深槽、钣金、结构件、焊接件这类常见工件。"

## Small Nozzle

- Diameter: 13 mm
- Best for: small deep grooves, recesses, small parts, and lighting fixtures.
- Typical parts: small stamped parts, die-cast parts, small grooves, small recessed areas, lighting parts.
- Use when: the target area is small, narrow, or requires more focused access.
- Caution: do not use it as a large-area productivity nozzle.

Chinese wording:

"Small Nozzle 更适合小深槽、小凹坑、小件和灯具类位置，不是拿来大面积铺粉的。"

## Round Head Nozzle

- Diameter: 30 mm
- Best for: high-volume structural products where finish requirements are not extremely critical.
- Typical parts: agricultural machinery, square tubes, guardrails, fence posts, and similar structural products.
- Use when: the customer values productivity and coverage on repeated structural parts.
- Caution: if the appearance requirement is very fine, confirm by test spraying.

Chinese wording:

"Round Head Nozzle 适合农业机械、方管、护栏、围栏桩这类批量结构件；如果外观要求特别高，要先试喷确认。"

## Selection Logic

If the user asks generally:

1. Ask for workpiece photos or geometry.
2. If it is a broad surface, suggest Flat Nozzle.
3. If it is a common cabinet/groove/structural part, suggest Long Rod Nozzle.
4. If it is a small groove, recess, or lighting part, suggest Small Nozzle.
5. If it is guardrail, square tube, agricultural machinery, or high-volume structural work, suggest Round Head Nozzle.

Do not claim one nozzle is best for every part. If the part has both large surfaces and recesses, mention that different nozzles or process steps may be used for different areas.

# Product Positioning

Use this reference for sales engineering language. Keep recommendations technical and honest.

## Positioning

Rotary cup powder coating equipment should be positioned as a professional option for automatic electrostatic powder coating lines where the customer values stable production, film uniformity, powder utilization, and repeatable process control.

## Value Propositions

Use these points when supported by the user's situation:

- Helps build a stable powder cloud for automatic coating.
- Can improve film uniformity on suitable workpieces.
- Can improve powder saving when the booth, grounding, powder recovery, and line setup are also correct.
- Can improve groove, recess, and difficult-area coverage on suitable workpieces after proper tuning.
- Can reduce dependence on manual spraying skill.
- Supports repeatable parameter tuning for production lines.

## Avoid Overclaiming

Do not claim:

- It solves every powder coating defect.
- It always saves a fixed percentage of powder.
- It is always better than spray guns.
- It can compensate for poor pretreatment, grounding, curing, or powder quality.

Use safer language:

- "It may be suitable if..."
- "This should be confirmed by test spraying..."
- "The main conditions to verify are..."
- "Rotary cup can help, but the root cause may also be..."

## Customer Education Language

Chinese:

"旋转杯头不是单纯换一个喷头就解决所有问题，它更适合配合稳定的自动线、良好的接地、合适的粉末输送和正确的喷涂路径一起使用。判断是否适合，要先看工件形状、产量、膜厚要求、现有线体和主要痛点。"

English:

"A rotary cup head is not just a drop-in fix for every coating problem. It works best as part of a controlled automatic line with good grounding, stable powder delivery, proper booth airflow, and a suitable motion path. Suitability depends on part geometry, output target, film thickness requirements, existing line condition, and the customer's main pain point."
